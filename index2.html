Manifesto

FURIOSO is built for readers who don’t want noise — they want signal. We publish slow, high-contrast dispatches: reporting that treats culture as evidence, not entertainment; geopolitics as lived reality, not distant theatre.

The goal is not to be “balanced”. The goal is to be accurate, sourced, and brave enough to name the mechanism.

How to read us

Start with the front page. Follow a series. Use search when you’re hunting a theme. If you value this work, keep it alive: support funds time — time funds reporting — reporting funds understanding.

ABOUT

Run-and-gun stories from the edges of the map.

FURIOSO covers global culture through the lens of power—conflict, history, money, borders, media—and how all of it leaks into music, fashion, language, food, and the way people live. Long reads. Sharp reporting. Documentary imagery. The aim isn’t hot takes. It’s understanding.

Built independent. Designed to feel like a magazine. Written to sound like a human being with skin in the game.
